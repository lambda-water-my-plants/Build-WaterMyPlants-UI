The Water My Plants team is comprised of some of the industries most dedicated developers and designers. Each works hard to ensure that your quality of life is improved by saving time and energy when it comes to keeping your plants healthy and fresh!

Meet The Team

The Water My Plants app is a designed and built from the ground up by a collaboration of students from different tracks at Lambda School.

Each team member has played a specific role in the app's creation in order to make your life easier and to showcase the various skills learned at Lambda.
