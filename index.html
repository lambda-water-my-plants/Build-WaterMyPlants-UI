- Water Your Plants -

The Water My Plants App ensures plant lovers never need to worry about scheduling or wondering about when the best time to water their plants are - we do it for you!

How It Works

Taking care of your plants has never been more simple!

With the Water My Plants app, all that you have to do is create an account and add all of the plants that you own.

We then create your own customized watering schedule based on the optimal time to water your plants for maximum health!

To make things even easier, we'll even send you text reminders to let you know when it is time to water!
